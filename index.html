§1 — On the self

About

I'm a student at Hongik University, currently working as an undergraduate researcher at PEM Lab, where I study deep learning. But the question that actually keeps me up is older than any architecture: what is understanding, and could something built rather than born ever have it?

I read Nietzsche for his suspicion of easy certainties, Donna Haraway for her refusal to draw clean lines between the natural and the made, and Zhuangzi for the oldest version of the question I keep asking about AI — if a mind can't be sure it isn't a butterfly dreaming it's a philosopher, what should that do to how confidently we build things we call "intelligent"?
